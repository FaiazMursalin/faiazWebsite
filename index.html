About Me

Hello, I am S.M. Faiaz Mursalin from Bangladesh currently working as a Graduate Teaching Assistant at the Department of Computer Science, Missouri State University side by side finishing my Masters in Computer Science focusing more on Data Science and Robotics. Previously I worked as a Software Engineer at BJIT Limited, Bangladesh. I designed full stack softwares focusing mainly on the Backend using Java(Spring, Spring Boot), Python(Flask, Django) and sometimes Frontend using React JS.

I obtained my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from BRAC University. Before that i finished my High School from Notre Dame College, Bangladesh. Moreover I worked as a Teaching Assistant at Computer Science and Engineering Department at Brac University in my final year of my undergraduate. Where I got the wonderful oppurtunity of guiding and teaching student Data Structure(CSE220).

My research interest focuses on Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Image Processing, Meta Learning, Predictive Learning, High-Performance Computing and Human Computer Interaction

Apart from research, I like to play Football and Basketball in my leisure times. I try to give tours every after 3 months mostly exploring new cities and hiking.
